Or perhaps you'd settle instead for a list of lines in which Shakespeare used the phrase "not found"?

  • The drug he gave me, which he said was precious
    And cordial to me, have I not found it
    Murderous to the senses? - Imogen in Cymbeline Act IV Scene 2

  • My good lord, have great care
    I be not found a talker. - Henry VIII in Henry VIII Act II Scene 2

  • If you will take a homely man's advice,
    Be not found here; hence, with your little ones. - Messenger in Macbeth Act IV Scene 2

  • I cry you mercy, sir; and well could wish
    You had not found me here so musical - Mariana in Measure for Measure ActIV Scene 1

  • To find persistive constancy in men:
    The fineness of which metal is not found
    In fortune's love; - Agamemnon in Troilus and Cressida Act I Scene 3

  • and the king shall live without an heir, if that
    which is lost be not found. - Officer in Winter's Tale Act III Scene 2
